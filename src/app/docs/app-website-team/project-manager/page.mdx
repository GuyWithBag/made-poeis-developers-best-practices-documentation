## ‚ùì What is Project Management?
Project management is the process of planning, executing, and closing projects. It involves coordinating resources, managing timelines, and ensuring that project goals are met efficiently and effectively. 

## üóùÔ∏è Key Components of Project Management
- **Planning**: Defining project goals, scope, and deliverables.
- **Execution**: Implementing the project plan, coordinating resources, and managing tasks.
- **Monitoring and Controlling**: Tracking project progress, managing changes, and ensuring quality.
- **Closing**: Finalizing all project activities, delivering the final product, and obtaining stakeholder approval.
- **Communication**: Keeping all stakeholders informed and engaged throughout the project lifecycle.
