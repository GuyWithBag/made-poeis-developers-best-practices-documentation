#### UI/UX Designer
- **Role**: Designs user-friendly and visually appealing interfaces.
- **Responsibilities**:
  - Creates wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications.
  - Collaborates with developers to ensure accurate implementation.
  - Conducts user research and usability testing.
  - **Example**: Designs an intuitive website navigation bar.
- **Sub-Roles**:
  - **Lead UI/UX Designer**: Sets design standards and oversees the team.
  - **Senior UI/UX Designer**: Leads design efforts and mentors juniors.
  - **Junior UI/UX Designer**: Assists with design tasks and learns.
- **Tools**: Figma, Adobe XD.
